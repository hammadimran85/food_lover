Restaurant App
Overview
Welcome to the Restaurant App, a comprehensive food ordering and delivery platform built with responsiveness in mind. This app allows users to browse food items, add them to cart, enter address, select payment method, and checkout. The app is designed to provide a seamless experience across various devices, including mobile, tablet, and web.
Features
Food Items: Browse through a variety of delicious food items with images, descriptions, and prices.
Add to Cart: Add food items to your cart and manage your order.
Address: Enter your address for delivery or pickup.
Payment Method: Select your preferred payment method, including credit cards, PayPal, and more.
Checkout: Complete your order and receive confirmation.
Responsiveness
The app is designed to be fully responsive, with separate layouts for:
Mobile: Optimized for small screens, with easy-to-use navigation and minimal scrolling.
Tablet: Designed for larger screens, with a more detailed layout and easy access to features.
Web: A full-featured web version, perfect for ordering from your desktop or laptop.
